Congrats Grad! Now what?

My idea for post-college life was to be a graphic designer. This ultimately happened, but not right away. First, I kept my two part-time jobs as a Copy and Print Specialist and Staples and a gymnastics coach alongwith an unpaid internship with the company I currently work for today. After about 6 months I accepted a job as an interior designer. Hated it. I never want to talk to that many people again within a month in my life. Eventually I was offered the Art Director position at the agency where I held an unpaid internship for about a year. I still work there today.

More Life (Drake said that)

I am one designer of two at the agency. For a little more than a year I was the only designer and occasionally had the help of a few interns. Glad I have a cohort now! In my spare time I workout a lot..like a lot a lot. I also take commissions under my own brand, EllPea Designs (for pronunciation purposes --> LP Designs). I paint using watercolor and acrylics, make digital illustrations, invitations, flyers, logos, etc. While I enjoy what I do, I would like more of a challenge as I think I will feel more fulfilled that way. This is why I am excited to participate in Covalence!
